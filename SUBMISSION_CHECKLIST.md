## Submission copy points

- CRAS governs permission before execution; it is not a better route planner.
- Evidence and authorization are one durable transaction.
- Unauthorized action cannot reach the robot adapter.
- The robot is a replaceable participant, not the source of authority.
- GPT-5.6 Codex enabled a philosopher/domain author to turn original inquiry-governance
  research into a tested browser, database, and physical-robot system during Build Week.
